Research

From neural circuits of stress-induced depression @ ZJU Brain Science Institute, to population genomics and human brain multi-omics of mood disorders @ FAHZU Psychiatry / Hu Lab.

Psychiatric Genomics

Dissecting the genetic basis of psychiatric disorders via multi-trait GWAS, facilitating clinical translation of polygenic risk scores.

Single-cell & Spatial Multi-omics

Integrating snRNA-seq, Stereo-seq, and scATAC-seq to map cell-type-specific dysregulation in postmortem bipolar disorder brain.
